## Market Competitiveness Logic
CPL scaling by market tier:
- **Tier 1 (NYC, LA, Chicago, SF):** Baseline × 1.4-1.6
- **Tier 2 (Phoenix, Dallas, Atlanta, Charlotte):** Baseline × 1.0-1.2
- **Tier 3 (Raleigh, Jacksonville, Columbus):** Baseline × 0.7-0.9
- **National:** Baseline × 1.0

This means a HVAC case study in NYC should show $180-220 CPL while one in Raleigh shows $90-130 CPL.
